Boeing 777X Aircraft Analysis

Analysis of a modern wide-body transport focusing on wing geometry, reference quantities, aerodynamic and structural estimates, and longitudinal/directional stability using DATCOM.

Overview

The project ties geometric data for the 777X-class configuration to classical analysis steps: locating the mean aerodynamic chord and aerodynamic center, then using numerical methods and DATCOM routines to comment on stability and trim-relevant quantities.

Technical work

  • Extracted and used wing geometry including MAC and aerodynamic center relationships.
  • Performed structural and aerodynamic calculations using course numerical methods.
  • Evaluated static and dynamic stability contributions with DATCOM.
