Jan 2026 – Jun 2026 · Senior Capstone — AIAA, Oregon State University

Active Roll Control System for Solid Rocket

Design of a servo-actuated trim tab integrated inside a 0.125 in fin to provide roll authority for a solid-propellant rocket, with CAD integration and aerodynamic validation.

Overview

This capstone addresses roll control for a solid rocket by packaging a trim-tab mechanism within the existing fin geometry. The work spans concept selection, detailed mechanical design, and analysis to verify that the system can generate sufficient rolling moment without unacceptable mass or reliability penalties.

Technical work

  • Developed a servo-actuated trim tab system integrated within a 0.125 in fin envelope.
  • Built and integrated component models in Onshape and Siemens NX for fit, interfaces, and drawings.
  • Performed aerodynamic analysis to estimate roll control authority and support design decisions.
  • Ran trade studies across eight or more concepts to balance mass, complexity, and reliability.
