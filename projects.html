Lunar Mission Rocket Design

AAE 462 — Rocket Propulsion, Oregon State University

Mission B design closed at 4.076 km/s total Δv with a two-stage architecture that reduced initial mass by 25.15%.

  • Designed for 50 kg delivery from 400 km LEO to 100 km circular low lunar orbit
  • Closed mass budget at 229.75 kg initial mass with 161.55 kg total propellant
  • Allocated 3.107 km/s (Stage 1) + 0.969 km/s (Stage 2) with restartable upper stage
  • Validated transfer/LOI performance with ~0.278 km/s residual Δv margin in the report trajectory case
